EHRI Persistent Identifiers

This service displays metadata about articles and other outputs created by the European Holocaust Research Infrastructure (EHRI) that have been assigned persistent identifiers (PIDs for short). EHRI currently uses DataCite to register Digital Object Identifiers (DOIs) for articles on the EHRI Document Blog, with more outputs to follow as per the EHRI PID Roadmap.

Please note: This service is currently under construction and will eventually contain information about EHRI PID policies.
